A rotating set of slides with full keyboard and screen-reader support. It autoplays, but pauses the moment you hover or focus it and can be stopped outright with the play/pause button (WCAG 2.2.2). Use the prev/next buttons, or Tab to the dots and arrow between them; paging wraps around (circular). On a touch screen you can also swipe the slides — pinch-zoom still works, and a swipe stops the autoplay rotation the same way the pause button does.
