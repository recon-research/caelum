Splitter

Drag a divider to resize — or focus it and use the arrow keys (Home / End snap to the min/max). The outer splitter is horizontal; its right pane nests a vertical splitter, so resizing composes. Each divider is a keyboard-operable role="separator". Both splitters carry a [stateKey], so your layout is remembered — resize, reload the page, and the panes come back the way you left them.
